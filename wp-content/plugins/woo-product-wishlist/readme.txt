== Description ==

WooCommerce Product Wishlist is a easy but effective tool that allow you to to transform your website traffic into dependable Customers. there are many situations in which clients can’t purchase a product presently or simply don’t want. possibility to save products for later encourages users to go back on your web site and in the end, make a buy. adding products to Wishlist is straightforward and convenient so it could additionally force your customers to shop for even greater merchandise than they planned. users can save their wishlist. Wishlist Extension growing Traffic of your website visitors, conversion and as a result sales.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Enable or disable whishlist from admin ? =
Yes, you can disable whishlist from woocommerce general setting.
